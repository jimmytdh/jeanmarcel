Services We Offered

Family Dispute Resolution

• Agreements are reached in a non-judgemental, balanced, respectful process managed by the Mediators.

• Impartiality is real, due to gender balancing, with a female & male Mediator, highly experienced, Master’s trained, with current National Accreditation as Family Dispute Resolution Practitioners (FDRP) and registered with NSW Attorney Generals Dept.

• I save you an immense amount of valuable time, heightened negative emotions, escalating conflicts and precious money.

• Efficient resolution of issues can be achieved, usually within 2 weeks of each Party’s confidential Initial Assessments, being much faster than through the family courts, some community organisations, all with exceedingly long waiting periods.

• Parents are assisted to focus on their children’s best interests, to craft reality tested resolutions, and make robust agreements, that all can live with, moving forward.

Workplace & Commercial Mediation

• Agreements are reached in a non-judgemental, balanced, respectful process managed by the Mediators

• Parties are assisted to focus on their needs and interests, to craft reality tested resolutions, and make robust agreements, that all can live with, moving forward

• Facilitated Discussions are often utilised for Team issues, when necessary

• Conciliation may be the best way to resolve a workplace or commercial issue and is highly effective and efficient

Investigation Service

• Efficient exploration of allegations and factual evidence gathering which allows for immediate safety as proper resolution of issues can be achieved. This is possible usually within 2 weeks of agreed Investigation Assignment. Your Policy and Code of Conduct documents are required and referred to, for each case.

• Accredited and registered with NSW and Victoria Police
