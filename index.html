About Jean Marcel

Compliance-focused, detail-oriented, and highly organised Mediator-Investigator-Trainer

Jean Marcel has an extensive experience in leading complex and sensitive situations as well as dispute management. His extensive research, superior critical thinking, and sound judgement facilitates evidence-based decision making to draw informed conclusions and recommendations. Applying his excellent attention to detail and written communication qualities, he prepare detailed and accurate submissions, case reports, statements, and briefs of evidence. Highly organised, he efficiently plan and prioritise competing workloads, completing tasks to resolution within tight timeframes.

With advanced verbal communication and interpersonal skills, Jean Marcel tailor his approach and language to build professional relationships with key stakeholders across all levels, including government agencies, health workers, legal professionals, as well as clients and their families. An effective and analytical thinker, he analyse multiple sources of information to implement effective processes and procedures parallel with legislative guidelines.
